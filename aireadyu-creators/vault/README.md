## 11 Creator Domains

| Domain | Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Content | `content/` | Scripts, articles, newsletters, content calendar, publishing schedules, video briefs |
| Brand | `brand/` | Style guides, brand assets, voice documentation, competitor analysis, brand guidelines |
| Monetization | `monetization/` | Revenue reports, membership tiers, merch catalog, course materials, pricing strategy |
| Analytics | `analytics/` | Performance reports, channel metrics, growth tracking, platform analytics exports |
| Audience | `audience/` | Community guidelines, newsletter subscriber data, engagement reports, feedback logs |
| Production | `production/` | Production schedules, asset libraries, editing templates, contractor briefs |
| Legal | `legal/` | Brand deal contracts, copyright registrations, DMCA notices, licensing agreements |
| Finance | `finance/` | Revenue statements, expense receipts, tax filings, sponsor invoices |
| Partnerships | `partnerships/` | Brand deal pipeline, sponsor contacts, rate cards, partnership agreements |
| Tools | `tools/` | Software subscriptions, equipment inventory, workflow documentation |
| Admin | `admin/` | Calendar records, travel docs, meeting notes, email templates |
