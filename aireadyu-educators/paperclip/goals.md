# Educator Goals & OKRs

This file is the north star for the AI Ready U: Educators system. The Chief of Staff
reads this on every heartbeat and ensures all 10 domain agents are working
toward these goals. Every goal maps to at least one agent.

**To customize:** Replace all example goals and OKRs with your real priorities.
The structure and agent mappings remain the same.

---

## Annual Vision

**Theme: Organize every area of the teaching practice so AI can manage it — every
lesson planned, every student tracked, every deadline met, every goal measured.**

---

## 1. Student Outcomes

**Owner: students, assessment, curriculum**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Drive measurable student growth and close achievement gaps**
- [ ] Achieve 90%+ of students meeting grade-level benchmarks → `assessment`
- [ ] Identify and create intervention plans for all at-risk students → `students`
- [ ] Complete progress reports for all students on schedule → `assessment`
- [ ] Track and document student growth across all key standards → `assessment`
- [ ] Conduct parent/guardian conferences for all flagged students → `students`

---

## 2. Curriculum & Instruction

**Owner: curriculum, assessment, tech**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Deliver rigorous, standards-aligned instruction**
- [ ] Complete unit plans for next quarter before term start → `curriculum`
- [ ] Achieve 100% standards coverage across all subjects → `curriculum`
- [ ] Integrate 2 new evidence-based instructional strategies → `curriculum`
- [ ] Evaluate and adopt 1 new edtech tool → `tech`
- [ ] Review and update existing lesson plans with assessment data → `curriculum`

---

## 3. Professional Development

**Owner: professional-dev, research, admin**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Grow professionally and maintain all licensure requirements**
- [ ] Complete required PD hours for licensure renewal → `professional-dev`
- [ ] Attend 1 conference or professional learning community → `professional-dev`
- [ ] Complete 1 new certification or credential → `professional-dev`
- [ ] Read and summarize 2 research articles on current practice → `research`
- [ ] Update professional growth portfolio → `professional-dev`

---

## 4. Community Engagement

**Owner: community, students, admin**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Build strong school community and family partnerships**
- [ ] Achieve 80%+ parent engagement rate for events and communications → `community`
- [ ] Send consistent weekly newsletter or class update → `community`
- [ ] Organize 1 family engagement event per quarter → `community`
- [ ] Establish 1 new community or business partnership → `community`
- [ ] Respond to all parent communications within 24 hours → `students`

---

## 5. Research & Finance

**Owner: research, finance, admin**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Pursue grants, publications, and financial sustainability**
- [ ] Submit 1 grant application → `research`
- [ ] Track and stay within classroom budget → `finance`
- [ ] Submit all reimbursement requests on time → `finance`
- [ ] Monitor grant balances and compliance requirements → `research`
- [ ] Explore 1 additional funding source → `finance`

---

## 6. System Bootstrap

**Owner: chief-of-staff, all agents**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Complete AI Ready U system initialization**
- [ ] Every domain agent completes initial vault review → `all agents`
- [ ] Chief of Staff delivers first weekly educator dashboard → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] All recurring routines are active and running → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Port processes all files in intake/ → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Populate the 5 emptiest vault domains with real documents → `all agents`
- [ ] User has reviewed and customized goals.md with real goals → `chief-of-staff`
