# Founders Goals & OKRs

This file is the north star for the AI Ready U: Founders system. The Chief of Staff
reads this on every heartbeat and ensures all 10 domain agents are working
toward these goals. Every goal maps to at least one agent.

**To customize:** Replace all example goals and OKRs with your real priorities.
The structure and agent mappings remain the same.

---

## Annual Vision

**Theme: Organize every area of the startup so AI can manage it — every domain
documented, every milestone tracked, every investor relationship nurtured.**

---

## 1. Product

**Owner: product, tech, customers**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Ship meaningful product progress toward PMF**
- [ ] Ship 1 major product milestone or feature this quarter → `product`
- [ ] Conduct user interviews with at least 10 customers → `product`
- [ ] Define and track 3 core PMF signal metrics → `product`
- [ ] Maintain a groomed backlog with at least 2 sprints of stories → `product`
- [ ] Complete competitive analysis and update positioning → `marketing`

---

## 2. Fundraising

**Owner: fundraising, legal, finance**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Advance the fundraising round**
- [ ] Schedule and complete meetings with 20+ target investors → `fundraising`
- [ ] Maintain a fully updated data room → `fundraising`
- [ ] Track round target, commitments, and close timeline → `finance`
- [ ] Ensure all term sheets and investment docs reviewed by legal → `legal`
- [ ] Send monthly investor update to all active prospects → `fundraising`

---

## 3. Team

**Owner: hiring, legal, finance**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Build and grow the right team**
- [ ] Fill all open roles within target timeline → `hiring`
- [ ] Maintain current job descriptions for all open positions → `hiring`
- [ ] Complete onboarding checklist for every new hire → `hiring`
- [ ] Ensure all employment agreements reviewed and signed → `legal`
- [ ] Track headcount budget against actual spend → `finance`

---

## 4. Revenue & Customers

**Owner: customers, marketing, product**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Grow ARR and deepen customer relationships**
- [ ] Achieve ARR target for the quarter → `customers`
- [ ] Onboard and activate all new customers within 30 days of close → `customers`
- [ ] Collect NPS from at least 80% of active customers → `customers`
- [ ] Produce 2 customer case studies → `marketing`
- [ ] Resolve top 3 customer-reported product issues → `product`

---

## 5. Operations

**Owner: operations, finance, tech**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Control runway and build operational foundation**
- [ ] Maintain burn rate at or below plan → `finance`
- [ ] Produce monthly financial close within 5 business days → `finance`
- [ ] Document all core operational processes as SOPs → `operations`
- [ ] Complete tool stack audit and eliminate redundant subscriptions → `operations`
- [ ] Maintain 18+ months of runway visibility → `finance`

---

## 6. System Bootstrap

**Owner: chief-of-staff, all agents**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Complete AI Ready U system initialization**
- [ ] Every domain agent completes initial vault review → `all agents`
- [ ] Chief of Staff delivers first weekly founders dashboard → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] All recurring routines are active and running → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Port processes all files in intake/ → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Populate the 5 emptiest vault domains with real documents → `all agents`
- [ ] User has reviewed and customized goals.md with real goals → `chief-of-staff`
