## 9 Brokerage Domains

| Domain | Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Clients | `clients/` | Client records, policy summaries, annual review notes, satisfaction tracking, referral follow-up logs |
| Renewals | `renewals/` | Renewal calendar, advance notice templates, re-marketing packages, retention rate reports |
| Intake | `intake/` | Prospect intake forms, needs analysis docs, risk profiles, application workflows, onboarding checklists |
| Quotes | `quotes/` | Quote requests, carrier comparison sheets, proposal drafts, coverage gap analyses |
| Claims | `claims/` | First notice of loss records, claims documentation, carrier follow-up logs, status trackers |
| Compliance | `compliance/` | License renewal records, E&O coverage docs, CE credit logs, state regulatory change notes |
| Carriers | `carriers/` | Carrier appointment docs, appetite guides, underwriter contacts, commission schedules |
| Marketing | `marketing/` | Referral partner outreach, niche content, social proof collection, lead generation records |
| Admin | `admin/` | Scheduling notes, policy document organization, email templates, administrative records |
