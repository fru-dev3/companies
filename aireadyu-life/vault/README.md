## 14 Life Domains

| Domain | Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Tax | `tax/` | Tax returns, W-2s, 1099s, receipts, CPA correspondence |
| Health | `health/` | Medical records, prescriptions, lab results, dental, vision |
| Wealth | `wealth/` | Bank statements, investments, retirement, benefits, credit reports |
| Career | `career/` | Resumes, certifications, reviews, offer letters, contracts |
| Legal | `legal/` | Wills, trusts, POA, identity docs, vital records, civic docs |
| Insurance | `insurance/` | Auto, health, life, home, umbrella, disability policies |
| Business | `business/` | LLC docs, contracts, filings, invoices, rental properties |
| Estate | `estate/` | Property records, deeds, mortgages, HOA, property tax |
| Home | `home/` | Warranties, appliances, utilities, maintenance, home inventory |
| Family | `family/` | Family docs, children, eldercare, shared finances, legacy |
| Learning | `learning/` | Degrees, transcripts, courses, certificates, CE records |
| Mind | `mind/` | Journal, notes, ideas, goals, therapy, relationships |
| Explore | `explore/` | Travel itineraries, bookings, hobbies, events, loyalty programs |
| Vehicles | `vehicles/` | Titles, registration, maintenance, warranties, recalls |
