## 10 REI Domains

| Domain | Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Properties | `properties/` | Property profiles, addresses, unit details, occupancy records, property tax bills |
| Acquisitions | `acquisitions/` | Deal pipeline, underwriting models, inspection reports, appraisals, comp analysis |
| Tenants | `tenants/` | Lease agreements, tenant applications, screening reports, rent rolls, correspondence |
| Maintenance | `maintenance/` | Work orders, vendor contracts, preventive maintenance schedules, inspection reports |
| Financing | `financing/` | Mortgage documents, loan applications, amortization schedules, refinance analyses |
| Taxes | `taxes/` | Depreciation schedules, cost segregation studies, Schedule E data, 1031 exchange docs |
| Insurance | `insurance/` | Landlord policies, commercial property insurance, flood insurance, claims |
| Legal | `legal/` | Purchase agreements, lease contracts, LLC formation docs, title reports, eviction filings |
| Accounting | `accounting/` | P&L statements, rent collection records, expense reports, NOI calculations, cash flow |
| Market | `market/` | Market reports, comp data, rent surveys, vacancy rate trends, economic indicators |
