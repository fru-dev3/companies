# AI Ready U: Recruiters

**11 AI agents. 10 recruiting domains. 12 skills. 1 Recruiting Operations Director. Private. On your machine.**

AI Ready U: Recruiters is a company of AI agents that manage your entire recruiting desk. Each agent is a domain expert — talent sourcing, candidate outreach, screening, pipeline tracking, client relations, job descriptions, offer management, onboarding, and business development. The Recruiting Operations Director orchestrates everything, monitors open requisitions, and keeps the candidate pipeline healthy.
