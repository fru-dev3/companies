# Small Business Goals & OKRs

This file is the north star for the AI Ready U: Small Business system. The Chief of Staff
reads this on every heartbeat and ensures all 12 domain agents are working
toward these goals. Every goal maps to at least one agent.

**To customize:** Replace all example goals and OKRs with your real priorities.
The structure and agent mappings remain the same.

---

## Annual Vision

**Theme: Organize every area of the business so AI can manage it — every domain
documented, every system automated, every goal tracked.**

---

## 1. Revenue Growth

**Owner: sales, marketing, products**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Accelerate revenue and expand market presence**
- [ ] Increase qualified pipeline by 25% over last quarter → `sales`
- [ ] Close 10 new accounts this quarter → `sales`
- [ ] Launch 2 demand-gen campaigns with measurable ROI → `marketing`
- [ ] Publish 8 pieces of content (blog, social, newsletter) → `marketing`
- [ ] Ship 1 major product feature or improvement → `products`
- [ ] Complete competitive analysis and update positioning → `marketing`

---

## 2. Customer Retention

**Owner: customers, sales, products**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Strengthen customer relationships and reduce churn**
- [ ] Achieve 90%+ customer retention rate → `customers`
- [ ] Complete quarterly business reviews with top 10 accounts → `customers`
- [ ] Collect and act on NPS feedback from 50+ customers → `customers`
- [ ] Identify and pursue 5 upsell opportunities → `sales`
- [ ] Resolve top 3 customer-reported product issues → `products`

---

## 3. Operational Efficiency

**Owner: operations, tech, vendors**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Streamline processes and reduce operational overhead**
- [ ] Document all core business processes as SOPs → `operations`
- [ ] Automate 3 manual workflows → `tech`
- [ ] Audit all vendor contracts and renegotiate where possible → `vendors`
- [ ] Reduce average task completion time by 15% → `operations`
- [ ] Complete technology stack audit and eliminate redundant tools → `tech`

---

## 4. Team & Culture

**Owner: hr, knowledge, admin**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Build a strong team and knowledge foundation**
- [ ] Complete hiring for all open positions → `hr`
- [ ] Launch onboarding playbook for new hires → `knowledge`
- [ ] Complete quarterly performance reviews → `hr`
- [ ] Build and maintain company knowledge base with 20+ articles → `knowledge`
- [ ] Organize 1 team event or offsite per quarter → `admin`

---

## 5. Compliance & Risk

**Owner: legal, finance, hr**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Ensure legal compliance and financial health**
- [ ] Review and update all active contracts → `legal`
- [ ] File quarterly taxes on time → `finance`
- [ ] Complete monthly financial close within 5 business days → `finance`
- [ ] Audit employee handbook for compliance with current regulations → `hr`
- [ ] Review and renew all business licenses and permits → `legal`
- [ ] Update privacy policy and terms of service → `legal`

---

## 6. System Bootstrap

**Owner: chief-of-staff, all agents**

### Quarterly OKRs

**Objective: Complete AI Ready U system initialization**
- [ ] Every domain agent completes initial vault review → `all agents`
- [ ] Chief of Staff delivers first weekly business dashboard → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] All recurring routines are active and running → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Port processes all files in intake/ → `chief-of-staff`
- [ ] Populate the 5 emptiest vault domains with real documents → `all agents`
- [ ] User has reviewed and customized goals.md with real goals → `chief-of-staff`
