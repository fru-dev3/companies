## 12 Business Domains

| Domain | Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Operations | `operations/` | SOPs, process maps, workflow docs, operational KPIs, quality standards |
| Customers | `customers/` | Customer records, support tickets, NPS surveys, onboarding checklists |
| Sales | `sales/` | Pipeline reports, proposals, contracts, pricing, revenue forecasts |
| Marketing | `marketing/` | Campaign plans, content calendar, brand guidelines, analytics reports |
| Finance | `finance/` | P&L statements, budgets, invoices, receipts, bank statements, tax filings |
| HR | `hr/` | Employee records, job descriptions, offer letters, handbook, reviews |
| Legal | `legal/` | Contracts, NDAs, terms of service, privacy policy, business licenses |
| Vendors | `vendors/` | Vendor contracts, SLAs, purchase orders, supplier evaluations |
| Products | `products/` | Product roadmaps, feature specs, user research, release notes |
| Knowledge | `knowledge/` | Knowledge base articles, playbooks, training docs, FAQs, templates |
| Tech | `tech/` | System architecture, software inventory, security policies, backup plans |
| Admin | `admin/` | Company directory, office policies, travel records, expense reports |
